SELF v SELFIE
The image is real. It is not complete.

You edit what others see.
You know what you leave out.

The question isn't why you edit.
The question is what repeated editing does to the person doing it.

This instrument asks you to write both versions — what you present and what you don't. The same prompts, two answers. At the end, the gap between them is read.

The Draft is not the real you. Both versions are constructed. The gap between them is what's interesting.

The Feed
What you present. The version you'd post, share, or show.
The Draft
What doesn't make it. The version you delete, hide, or don't say.
